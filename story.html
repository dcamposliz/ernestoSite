STORY

Born in San Diego, California.

Raised in the slums of Tijuana, Mexico.

Obsessed with learning, building things, and competing.

Launched first business at age 10, bought a computer with profits.

Learned English at age 15.

Crossed the US - Mexico international border
for 3 years to attend high school in San Diego, California.

Moved to the United States for college.

Earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics & Spanish
at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

Studied abroad in Spain
at the Complutense University of Madrid.

Completed Professional Certificate in Technology Entrepreneurship
through UCSB Extension & UCSB's College of Engineering.

Founded Napses: first technology startup while in college.

Launched revenue-generating mvp to over 100 customers.

Startup experiment failed after about 7 months in production.
Our team needed more experience building things!

After all that jazz, I realized I love designing & building things,
so I spend a lot of my time learning, designing, & building.
